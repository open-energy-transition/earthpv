What OSM already knows, and what the model just found

Every blue point is a rooftop or ground-mount solar array already mapped in OpenStreetMap — the labels this project trained on. Every amber point is a candidate the fine-tuned model detected on its own pass over Pakistan's Sentinel-2 composites. The two rarely coincide: most of OSM's mapping sits in a handful of well-surveyed cities, while the model sees the whole imaged country — which is the point of a recall-first detector feeding a human-validation queue, not a metered count.
